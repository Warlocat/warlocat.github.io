Video games made by Chaoqun

I made a few video games with RPG Maker and other game engines. These games are in their very early stage and may not be smooth when playing. I put them here just for fun after realizing that video games made by RPG Maker are actually html- and js-based so that they can be played online.

北中一梦 (chinese version only) This is a puzzle game with some horror elements based on my high school 山东省北镇中学. Although I do like my classmates and (most of) my teachers at my high school, I have to admit that this game mostly reflects my negative opinions towards the school and the educational system behind it.
